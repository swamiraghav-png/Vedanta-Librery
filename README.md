🌿 Living Vedanta Library
Welcome

Welcome to the Living Vedanta Library, a humble digital sanctuary dedicated to the timeless wisdom of Advaita Vedānta and the sublime teachings of Yoga Vāsiṣṭha.

This library is a lifelong offering by Swami Raghabananda, preserving reflections, bilingual Satsangh posts, contemplative essays, banners, and spiritual insights inspired by the eternal dialogue between Sage Vasiṣṭha and Sri Rama.

Vision

The purpose of this library is simple:

To preserve the wisdom of Vedānta in an accessible form.
To present Yoga Vāsiṣṭha with clarity and contemplation.
To share Hindi and English reflections for seekers of different backgrounds.
To create a living archive where ancient wisdom continues to inspire modern hearts.

Guiding Spirit

This library is inspired by the timeless vision that:

The world is not created—it is merely a shimmer in Consciousness.

And:

मन ही विश्व का चित्रकार है; जगत् उसका चित्र।

The mind is the painter of the universe; the world is its painting.

A Personal Note

I have walked through life as a seeker, a student of Vedānta, a worker in the world, and a lover of silence.

These pages are not offered as declarations of scholarship, but as humble reflections gathered through years of study, contemplation, and satsang.

If these writings bring peace, clarity, or a moment of inner stillness to even one heart, this effort shall be fulfilled.

With reverence,

Swami Raghabananda
